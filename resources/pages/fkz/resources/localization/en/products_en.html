Our Range of Products

On this page you can find generally what we can produce and what our capabilities are. If you have any special requirements or need further clarification feel free to send us a inquiry.

The online price list is not available, for all necessary information please contact our Sales Department.

Our scope:

  • Drawn and annealed copper wire, diameter from 0,15 to 4,52 mm.
  • Drawn aluminium wire, diameter from 1,3 to 5,0 mm.
  • Tinned copper wire, drawn and annealed, diameter from 0,20 to 3,00 mm.
  • Copper and aluminium ropes, round and compacted, cross-section from 16 do 630 sqmm.
  • Flexible ropes consisting of tinned or untinned copper wire, cross-section from 10 do 185 sqmm.
  • Thermoplastic material insulated power installation conductors, cross-section from 0,50 do 16,00 sqmm
  • Thermoplastic material insulated power cables, for nominal voltages up to 10 kV, cross-section from 1,5 to 500 sqmm.
  • Thermoreactive material insulated power cables for nominal voltages up to 35 kV, cross-section from 25 to 400 sqmm.
  • Cables for oil industry, cross-section from 6 to 35 sqmm.
  • PVC insulated signal and control cables, cross-section from 1,5 to 4 sqmm.
  • Natural or synthetic rubber insulated mining cables for nominal voltages up to 10 kV, cross-section from 0,5 to 95 sqmm.
  • Natural or synthetic rubber insulated flexible conductors, cross-section from 0,5 to 6 sqmm.
  • Silicone rubber insulated heat resistant flexible conductors.
  • Flexible spiralized or non-spiralized conductors for household electrical appliances.
  • Conductors and cables for special purposes.
  • Thermoplastic material insulated telecommunication cables and conductors.
  • Coaxial and CATV-cables and conductors.
  • Composite power-signal conductors and cables with different capacity.
  • Fire resistant and halogen free conductors and cables.
  • Designing and engineering services in the field of cable and cable equipment production technology.
