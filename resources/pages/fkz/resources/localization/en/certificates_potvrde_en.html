  Cable Factory Zajecar D.O.O. boasts many product ratings. Certificates intended for the domestic market or Certificates of conformity are issued by independent bodies Quality A.D. Niš or the Vinča Institute of Nuclear Sciences.

Certificates of conformity, in accordance with the Directive, apply and are issued only for installation conductors and power cables with a rated voltage up to and including 0.6 / 1kV. Medium - voltage cables, rated voltage above 0.6 / 1kV are not covered by this Directive and they are not issued a Certificate of Conformity.
