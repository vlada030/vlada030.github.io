History

TF Kable Fabrika kablova Zajecar d.o.o. was founded in 1974. The Factory was an integral part of the Holding Company - The Mining and Smelting Combine BOR Group. From April 2008. factory owner is Tele-Fonika Kable Sp. z o.o. S.K.A. from Poland.

The development of the Factory could be divided in several phases:

  • 1974. Starts the production of PVC insulated power installation, signal and control and flexible conductors and cables for nominal voltages up to 1 kV;
  • 1978. Significant extension of production capacity and production assortment was accomplished, with: aluminium cables, XLPE insulated cables for nominal voltages up to 35 kV, rubber insulated and sheathed cables, power cables with concentric copper conductor (NYCWY cables);
  • 1994. New production section was set to work for production of PVC, polyethylene and cellular polyethylene insulated telecommunication conductors and cables, with production capacity of approximately 1500 t per year;
  • 1999. Extension of production range was accomplished by mastering technology for production of fire resistant and halogen-free cables, as well as data cables;
  • 2002. Plant for production of PVC granules for insulating and sheathing cables was set to work. Capacity of this plant is approximately 3.000 tones per year.

Production capacity of Factory is about 20.000 tones of cables and conductors per year. Our products meet the requirements of worldwide standards and norms such as: JUS, IEC, BS, VDE, HD, NFC, GOST, SS, SABS, BDS. Our products are exported in large number of countries worldwide.

In 1995 TF Kable Fabrika kablova Zajecar d.o.o. implemented Quality Management System in accordance with ISO 9002 standard and in 2006 implemented Environment Management System in accordance with ISO 14001:2004 standard. Now Factory has Quality Management System in accordance with standard ISO 9001:2015, Certificate Number DAS83378781/1/Q and Environment Management System in accordance with standard ISO 14001:2015, Certificate Number DAS93187253/1/E.

Our slogan "QUALITY AND RECOGNITION ARE ON OUR SIDE" is followed by conquering new products and markets.

Lasting determination of the Factory is maximum in satisfying all customers requirements and development of successful business cooperation with partners.
